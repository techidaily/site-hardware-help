## Personalize Your Device: Tutorial for Crafting a Custom Lock Screen on the Google Pixel

Google Pixel phones are known for their simplicity, but simplicity can come with a lack of customization options. Thankfully, [Android 14](https://youtube-blog.techidaily.com/24-uniting-subscribers-through-shared-playlists/) introduced some much-needed lock screen personalization. We'll show you how to change the clock style, font, size, and colors.

 When it comes to personalization, Pixel phones have greatly [lagged behind the likes of Samsung Galaxy devices](https://some-skills.techidaily.com/transformative-tools-unveiling-top-6-nft-platforms-for-artists-for-2024/). Sure, the [Material You theming is cool](https://extra-guidance.techidaily.com/plot-craft-guild-bestowed-batch-for-2024/), but the actual design choices have mostly been left up to Google. The new lock screen features in Android 14 put more control in your hands.

 First, swipe down twice from the top of the screen and select the gear icon to open the Settings.

![Tap the gear icon.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/screenshot_20231016-144758.png) 

 Next, go to the "Wallpaper & Style" section.

![Go to the "Wallpaper & Style" section.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/screenshot_20231016-144824.png) 

 "Lock Screen" should already be selected at the top of the screen, but make sure it is if not.

![Select "Lock Screen."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/screenshot_20231016-144903.png) 

 The first thing we can do is choose a clock style. Swipe from left to right to cycle through the different options.

![Swipe through the clock styles.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/screenshot_20231016-145005.png) 

 Once you're happy with your clock choice, tap "Clock Color & Size."

![Tap "Clock Color & Size."](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/2023-10-16_15-26-14.png)

 Here's where you can choose the specific color of the clock. The first color in the row will keep it tied to the overall theme of the phone. If you choose a different color, you can also adjust its brightness.

![Clock color options.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/screenshot_20231016-145145.png) 

 The "Size" tab gives you the option to have a "Dynamic" clock that will grow and shrink to accommodate notifications, or a permanent "Smal" clock.

![Clock size options.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/screenshot_20231016-145224.png)
