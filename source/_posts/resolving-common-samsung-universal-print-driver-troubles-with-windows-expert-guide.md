1. On your keyboard, press the **Windows logo key![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/09/img_59b0b16974940.png)**  and**R** at the same time to invoke the**Run** box.
2. Type**devmgmt.msc** and click**OK** .
