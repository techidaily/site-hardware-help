## Unveiling the Logitech Keys-To-Go 2: Initial Thoughts and In-Depth Product Analysis

The Keys-To-Go 2 is a new compact and portable Bluetooth keyboard offering from Logitech, designed to be used with the iPad. The latest version has a key that lets you cycle quickly through paired devices, and Logitech claims three years of battery life from its coin cell batteries.
