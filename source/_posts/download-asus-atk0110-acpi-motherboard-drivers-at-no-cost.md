**1)** Go to the **[Logitech website](https://tools.techidaily.com/drivereasy/download/)**  , then click**SUPPORT > Downloads** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/2019-12-06_11-33-28-1024x478.jpg)
