### Key Takeaways

 Visit Amazon's Appstore download page, download the APK file, and open it. Grant permission to "install unknown apps" if prompted. Finally, tap "Install" to install the Amazon Appstore.

 Android devices almost exclusively come with the Google Play Store for downloading apps and games. However, if you want to [use Android without a Google account](https://screen-capture.techidaily.com/new-2024-approved-beyondbaselinecameras-whats-upnext/), there are alternatives. One popular option is the Amazon Appstore.

 The Amazon Appstore is the app store that's available on [Amazon Fire tablets](https://video-screen-grab.techidaily.com/updated-elite-recording-tech-top-10-cam-devices-windows-11-for-2024/), Fire TVs, [and Windows 11](https://fake-location.techidaily.com/what-are-location-permissions-life360-on-vivo-y27-4g-drfone-by-drfone-virtual-android/). It's much smaller than the Play Store, but it's easily the next best choice. We'll show you how to install it on any Android device.

 We will be [sideloading](https://facebook-video-content.techidaily.com/updated-unlock-premium-quality-streaming-on-the-worlds-largest-network/) the Amazon Appstore. To begin, open a web browser such as Google Chrome on your Android device and head to [Amazon's Appstore download page](https://www.amazon.com/gp/mas/get/amazonapp?tag=htg-v2-74fj8bi-20&ascsubtag=UUhtgUeUpU203159&asc%5Frefurl=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.howtogeek.com%2F856663%2Fhow-to-install-the-amazon-appstore-on-an-android-phone%2F&asc%5Fcampaign=Short-Term).
