## Effective Methods to Determine High-Space Consuming Apps on Your Android Phone

We typically think of photos, videos, and music as the things that [take up space on our Android phones](https://facebook-videos.techidaily.com/updated-recorded-reality-facebook-live-2023/). However, apps and games require precious storage space too. We'll show you how to see which apps use the most.

 It's easy to see which of your apps are using the most storage space right from the Google Play Store. To get started, open the Play Store and tap your profile icon in the search bar.

![Tap your profile icon.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/2021-12-28_16-59-08.jpg)
